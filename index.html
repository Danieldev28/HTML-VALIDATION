Cookies and Opting-Out


sunnycommunities.com uses cookies, a small data file created by our dynamic website through your web browser, and is saved on your device either temporarily for that session only or permanently on the device’s memory storage hardware. Cookies allow Sunny Communities to keep track of your preferences to allow us to offer you individual and personalized service on website pages and commercial electronic communications such as email, as mentioned above.

In addition to cookies, our website may use session variables, web beacons/tracking tags, other technologies and third-party software services to provide you with unique individual experiences.

Based on the digital devices or software you use to access sunnycommunities.com, you may be given the option to disable cookies and other technology that may identify you, as well as to clear any previously gathered data. Consult your user guide or other resources on how to do this, but please be aware that by doing so, you will no longer benefit from the personalization of service we can provide with the service. You may not be able to use all the features of our website, store your preferences, our webpages may not display properly and you may not receive personalized and timely commercial electronic messages with specialized, high-value offers and deals.
